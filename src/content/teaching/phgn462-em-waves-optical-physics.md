## The course

PHGN 462 develops the wave description of light from solutions of the electromagnetic wave equation: plane and guided waves, refraction, interference, diffraction, and polarization, with applications in imaging, lasers, optical resonators, and waveguides. It is the upper-division optics course in the Engineering Physics curriculum and builds on intermediate electromagnetism.
