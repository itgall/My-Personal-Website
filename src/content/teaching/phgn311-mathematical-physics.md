## The course

PHGN 311 introduces the mathematical toolkit that recurs throughout physics, showing how linear algebra, complex variables, Fourier analysis, and vector calculus tie together problems in mechanics, quantum mechanics, optics, and electricity and magnetism.

## My role

I provided supplemental lectures, review sessions, and office hours, wrote original exam and problem-set material, and mentored more than fifty undergraduates.
